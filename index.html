Why Should I Learn about Cyber Security?

Have you ever thought about how much information you have shared on the internet? Bank account and routing numbers, social security numbers, full names and dates of birth, etc. These are a few of the most important numbers to keep private and not allow in the wrong hands. Sharing this information is common over the interent and hackers can easily take advantage if you are not careful. Understanding the different techniques hackers will use, and learning how to prevent your information from being stolen are both essential in this world of technology.
