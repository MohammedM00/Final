Methods to Prevent Cyber Attacks

HOME PREVENTION COMMON ATTACKS

Multi-factor authentication

This could include a text message to your phone everytime you login to make it more difficult for an attacker.

Verifying links and thinking twice before clicking

Many times, attackers will send a convincing email with a link to access your information. Take a moment to analyze the email and content before clicking

Updating your software and settting the updates to automatic

Software is not foolproof and updates are constantly being made to fix security issues and other errors. If you do not update software, attackers will take advantage of your out of date technology and security

Creating strong passwords

This sounds like a no brainer but the longer, more complex and unique your password, the more difficult it will be for attackers to access your information
